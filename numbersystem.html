The trick is to repeat 1 and 8 (n-1) times where n is the number of times 3 is repeated.

$$(33)^2=1089$$

$$(333)^2=110889$$

To know whether a number is Prime or not ?

First of all calculate the approximate square root of the number. Now, Divide the number by the prime numbers lesser than the approximate square root value. If the given number is not divisible by any of these number, then the number is Prime. If the number is divisible by any of the number then its Not Prime.

Eg: To calculate whether 367 is prime or not?

Approximate square root is 19. So Prime numbers less than 19 are 2,3,5,7,11,13,17. 367 is not divisble by 2,3,5,7,11,13,17; Hence 367 a prime number.

To calculate Sum of N natural Number

For starters Whole Numbers start from 0 & Natural Number start from 1.

Sum of n natural numbers: $$\frac{n(n+1)}{2}$$

Sum of n even number: $${n(n+1)}$$
