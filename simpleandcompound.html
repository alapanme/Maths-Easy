Simple and Compound Interest
Calculating Simple Interest

If 'p' is the Principal, 'r' is the rate per annum and 't' is the time in years, then Simple Interest will be calculated as:

$$\frac{p\times r\times t}{100}$$

Calculating Profit & Loss on the basis of Cost Price & Selling Price

If 'p' is the Principal,'r' is the rate per annum and 'n' is the number of years, then Compound Interest will be calculated as:

$$p\left[\left(1+\frac{r}{100}\right)^n-1\right]$$
