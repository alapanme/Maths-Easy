Time Distance & Work
Work and Days

If a person can do a/b part of the work in 'x' days and c/d part of the work in 'y' days then:

$$\frac{x}{a/b}=\frac{y}{c/d}$$

Water Filled and emptied in Cistern

If a cistern takes 'x' minutes to be filled by a pipe, but due to a leak it takes 'y' extra minutes to get filled, then the time taken by leak to empty the cistern will be calculated as:

$$(\frac{x^2+xy}{y}) min$$
