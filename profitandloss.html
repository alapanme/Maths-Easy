Profit & Loss
Calculating Profits on the basis of Marked Price and Cost Price

Profit Percent will be calculated as:

$$\frac{(M.P-C.P)\times100}{C.P}$$

Calculating Profit & Loss on the basis of Cost Price & Selling Price

If CP of 'x' things=SP of 'y' things then Profit/Loss% will be calculated as:

$$\left[\frac{(x-y)}{y}\times100\right]$$

If Positive then Profit and if Negative then Loss.
